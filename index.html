FAQs ?

Frequently Asked Questions



Q1: What languages can my child learn with LanguageKids?
A1: We offer a variety of languages including Spanish, French, Mandarin, German, and more. Check our course listings for the full range of options.

Q2: What age group is LanguageKids suitable for?
A2: Our platform is designed for school children aged 5-14. We offer age-appropriate content that grows with your child.

Q3: How does the interactive learning work?
A3: We use a combination of games, songs, videos, and interactive exercises to make learning engaging. Kids earn rewards and badges as they progress, keeping them motivated.

Q4: Can parents track their child's progress?
A4: Yes, parents can monitor their child's progress through our intuitive dashboard, which provides insights into lesson completion and areas needing improvement.

Q5: Are the lessons live or pre-recorded?
A5: We offer both live lessons with certified tutors and pre-recorded sessions that children can access at their convenience.

Q6: How do I get started?
A6: Simply sign up on our website, choose the language your child wants to learn, and start exploring our interactive lessons today.
